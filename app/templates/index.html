Instructions

We are gathering data to improve the speech recognition in Let's Go, an automated bus route information system. We need you to listen to 26 clips of speech from actual callers and write out what you think they said {%if hard%} and any other sounds they make, or background noises that can be heard {%endif%}. Some of the clips may be incomplete.

{%if hard %} Please mark incomplete words or stuttering with a dash at the start or end of the word, e.g. "I’m go- I’m going to South Side Works." Also, indicate background noises in brackets, e.g. "[cars passing by] Forbes and Craig to Sixth and Smithfield." {%else%} Please ignore any incomplete words, hesitations like “um," or stuttering. Only write down complete words, and ignore any background noises or throat clearing, etc. {%endif%}

Capitalization and punctuation don't matter as long as the words are clearly labelled. Please write place names as fully as possible, e.g. "Fifth Avenue" rather than "5th Ave." If you are unsure of a word, please make your best guess or use three questions marks (???).
