Instructions

We are gathering data to improve the speech recognition in Let's Go, an automated bus route information system. We need you to listen to 26 clips of speech from actual callers and write out what they said{%if hard%}, any sounds they made, and any background noises that can be heard{%endif%}. We recommend listening with headphones. Note that some of the clips may be incomplete.

{%if hard %} Please mark incomplete words or stuttering with a dash at the start or end of the word, e.g. "I'm go- I'm going to South Side Works." Indicate background noises in brackets with a description of the sound, e.g. "[cars passing by] Forbes and Craig to Sixth and Smithfield". Following are the tags for background noises that you may use:
1. [car horn]
2. [cough]
3. [cry]
4. [noise]

Please write bus numbers as one word, e.g. "61D". If you are unsure of a word, please make your best guess or use three questions marks , e.g. "???".
